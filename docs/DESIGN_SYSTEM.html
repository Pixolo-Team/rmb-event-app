Color

A trust-blue anchor (same family MSME owners already associate with professional networks) paired with one warm accent reserved only for live-event energy — the leaderboard, the scan button, streaks. Semantic colors for status never borrow the accent.

09

Accessibility & performance

Every rule here traces back to a line in the PRD, not a generic checklist.

  • 44×44px minimum tap targets, 52px on primary CTAsRadha is time-constrained and one-handed; mis-taps cost her the ninety seconds she has with the person in front of her.
  • 16px minimum body text, 4.5:1 contrast minimumBudget Android screens + bright venue lighting + a 35–55 audience. Never rely on color alone — status pills always pair a color with an icon or label.
  • Offline state is a first-class UI state, not an errorDirectory, matches, check-in and scanning all render fully from cache; the warning-100 banner explains what's degraded rather than blocking the screen.
  • <500KB initial bundle, <3s load on 4GFonts are variable (two files cover 5 weights), icons are inline SVG with no icon-font request, photos auto-compress under 100KB.
  • No feature requires a photoAvatars default to initials on brand-100 — MSME owners without a professional headshot are still fully represented in the directory and leaderboard.
10

Voice & content

Evento talks like the person standing next to Radha at the check-in desk, not like software. Short sentences, a first name when we have one, and a next step in every message.

Do

"Welcome! You're checked in at 9:15 AM"

"You met Deepak Sharma from TechCorp! View their profile."

"Can't check in. Try again or ask staff to scan your QR."

Don't

"Check-in successful. Geolocation verified."

"Contact record created and linked to meeting entity."

"An error occurred during the check-in process."
